Overview

The AI Boot Camp is an intensive, fast-paced program designed to quickly build your skills in artificial intelligence. This comprehensive program covers essential topics in AI prompting, AI models, AI workflows, GPTs, and Python for AI.

Whether you're a complete beginner or have some programming experience, this boot camp will provide you with the foundational knowledge and practical skills needed to start working with AI technologies. Through a combination of theory and hands-on exercises, you'll learn how to leverage AI tools and techniques to solve real-world problems.
